League Regulations

Match Logistics

The Matchday Window is strictly 24 hours. Managers are expected to initiate contact within the first 6 hours of the window opening. Failure to schedule a match without a valid "Force Majeure" report will result in a walk-over (3-0) loss.

Connection Integrity

In the event of a Network Disconnect before the 80th minute, the match must be restarted to play the remaining time. The previous scoreline is cumulative. Any disconnect after 85:00 is considered a finished match unless the goal difference is ≤ 1.

Admin Protocol

Managers have the right to autonomy in playstyle; however, Excessive red cards (3+ in a single match) will trigger an automatic review by the Judicial Committee for "Unsportsmanlike Conduct."

Evidence Submission

Results are only ratified upon the submission of Visual Evidence (Screenshots) to the Results Room. Both the Scoreline and the Match Statistics page must be captured. Reports must be filed within 30 minutes of match completion.


Mandatory Compliance: Ignorance of these laws is not an excuse. Violation of any article below may result in immediate point deductions, transfer bans, or league expulsion.
