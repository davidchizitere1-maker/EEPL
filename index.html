About

DAYYVIID ENTITIES is an independent game production house dedicated to redefining digital experiences. We specialize in high-fidelity simulations that prioritize fluid physics, tactical depth, and immersive atmosphere. We operate at the intersection of imagination and digital reality, founded on the principle that gaming is the ultimate form of human expression.

We have dedicated our studio to the architecture of complex environments and the souls that inhabit them. We do not view ourselves merely as developers, but as curators of experience. Our studio was born from a desire to move past the "finite"—to create systems where player agency and narrative depth collide to create something that feels truly alive.

The Vision

Led by producer David Ike-Ineh, our mission is to put the power of world-class mechanics directly into the hands of the global gaming community. We operate with a commitment to technical excellence, driven by the belief that a single world can hold infinite possibilities.

Our vision is to democratize high-tier gaming experiences, ensuring that every player, regardless of their location, has access to the most responsive, visceral, and polished gameplay imaginable. We don't follow industry trends; we hunt for the "infinite" in every concept, pushing the boundaries of what a small, dedicated team can manifest on a global scale.

Our Creations

Every creation is a labor of passion and a masterpiece, designed to immerse players in worlds that challenge and inspire. We refer to our users as Entities, and our project a self-sustaining digital world with their own rules, physics, and heartbeat.

We prioritize "Atmospheric Integrity," ensuring that every creation offers a level of immersion that transcends the screen.

Our flagship project Zero: Extraction . A high-octane fighting game designed for precision and cinematic action. It serves as the benchmark for our commitment to fluid physics and tactical depth.
